# IDDO-SDTM Tutorial Treatments and Interventions (IN) Domain

A fictional IN dataset for testing code and training

## Usage

``` r
IN_RPTESTB
```

## Format

### 

A data frame with 1 row and 27 columns:

- STUDYID:

  Study Identifier

- DOMAIN:

  Domain Abbreviation

- USUBJID:

  Unique Subject Identifier

- INSEQ:

  Sequence Number

- INTRT:

  Reported Name of Drug or Intervention

- INMODIFY:

  Modified Reported Term

- INDECOD:

  Standardized Drug or Intervention Name

- INCAT:

  Category for Drug or Intervention

- INPRESP:

  Drug or Intervention Pre-specified

- INOCCUR:

  Drug or Intervention Occurrence

- INDOSE:

  Dose per Administration

- INDOSEU:

  Dose Units

- INDOSFRM:

  Dose Form

- INPSTRG:

  Pharmaceutical Strength

- INPSTRGU:

  Pharmaceutical Strength Units

- VISITNUM:

  Visit Number

- VISIT:

  Visit Name

- VISITDY:

  Planned Study Day of Visit

- EPOCH:

  Epoch

- INSTDY:

  Study Day of Start of Drug or Intervention

- INENDY:

  Study Day of End of Drug or Intervention

- INDUR:

  Duration

- INRPOC:

  Reported Observation Occurrence

- INSTRF:

  Time Point Reference

- INEVINTX:

  Evaluation Interval Text

- IDOSIND:

  Total Dose Indicator

- INDY:

  Study Day of Intervention
